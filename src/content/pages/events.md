## Event Guidelines

Events listed here should include confirmed dates, times, locations, accessibility notes, and clear points of contact. Replace placeholder events with approved calendar information before launch.

## Welcoming Participation

Public events should communicate who the event is for, what participants can expect, and how families, youth, volunteers, or partner organizations can take part.
