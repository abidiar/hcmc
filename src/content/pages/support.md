## Supporting the Council

This page is designed to explain support opportunities without operating as a donation processor. Add approved instructions for contributions, sponsorships, in-kind support, or fiscal procedures only after they are confirmed by organizational leadership.

## Support May Include

- Sponsoring public programs or service initiatives.
- Contributing supplies for community events or assistance efforts.
- Offering professional services or meeting space.
- Connecting the council with aligned partners and civic institutions.

## Important Note

Do not place credit card forms, payment links, donor databases, or financial workflows on this site unless the organization later chooses an approved third-party platform and completes the required review.
