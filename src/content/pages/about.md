## Mission

Howard County Muslim Council supports community connection, public service, education, and civic engagement for the benefit of Howard County residents. The council seeks to strengthen relationships across communities while reflecting the values of compassion, dignity, and shared responsibility.

## Values

- Service that responds to real local needs.
- Respectful dialogue across faiths, cultures, and civic perspectives.
- Youth and family engagement that builds confidence and belonging.
- Partnerships that improve community outcomes.
- Clear public communication and responsible stewardship.

## Community Role

The council can serve as a bridge between residents, faith communities, civic organizations, schools, service providers, and public agencies. Its public role is to help people find trusted information, meaningful opportunities to serve, and constructive ways to participate in local life.
