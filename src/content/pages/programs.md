## Program Approach

Programs should be practical, welcoming, and easy to understand. Each program description can be updated in its own Markdown file under the programs content folder.

## How Programs Are Selected

Program priorities may reflect community listening, partner input, local needs, available volunteer capacity, and alignment with the council's civic and service mission.

## Partnership Opportunities

Schools, nonprofits, houses of worship, civic associations, and public agencies are encouraged to contact the council about appropriate partnership opportunities.
