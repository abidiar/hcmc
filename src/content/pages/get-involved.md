## Ways to Participate

Community members can help by volunteering at events, joining service projects, mentoring youth, supporting outreach, contributing professional skills, or introducing the council to aligned partner organizations.

## Volunteer Principles

- Be respectful of all participants and community partners.
- Follow event instructions and safety expectations.
- Represent the organization with care, humility, and reliability.
- Protect personal information and avoid speaking on behalf of the council unless authorized.

## Partner With Us

Organizations interested in partnership can share a short description of the proposed collaboration, intended audience, timeline, and desired outcomes through the contact page.
